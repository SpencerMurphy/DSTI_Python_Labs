QuelFruit

The Fruit Identification AI

This software uses YOLO object detection to identify fruits you might find in the grocery store. With a simple thumbs up, thumbs down gesture the customer can confirm the ID and print their barcode, saving them time and the need to touch a potentially dirty screen
